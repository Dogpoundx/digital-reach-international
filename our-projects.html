Our Projects

Our projects include sourcing and donating IT equipment, training and teaching in affiliate schools, community centres, and in our head offices in each community. We pull together the expertise of volunteers from advanced digital backgrounds and deploy these resources for the benefit of young people in various disadvantaged communities.
