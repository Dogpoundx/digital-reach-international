About Us

Our passion as Africans living in a digitalised western world is to set a platform through which young Africans who are passionate about digital education can learn and thrive in various IT fields.

Digital Reach is an educational not-for-profit community developmental initiative within the information technology and digital transformation space. Our goal is to ensure that disadvantaged communities and people are not left behind in the global digital revolution.

Our Mission

Our mission is to actively assist in sourcing materials, equipment, and expertise to help enhance and make digital education accessible to the most disadvantaged communities both in the western world and across communities in Africa.
